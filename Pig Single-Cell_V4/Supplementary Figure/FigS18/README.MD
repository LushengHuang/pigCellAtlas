## Plot figures for Figure S18

1. Figure_S18A.r: Plot the boxplot showing the differences in the body weight across NW and GR groups.
2. Figure_S18_G_plot.R: This script visualized the differential expression levels of 44 actin and myosin-encoding genes in the  type II myofiber C1 subcluster between normal-weight (NW) and growth-restricted (GR) group. 
3. Figure_S18_H_plot.R: Visualization of the amino acid composition of the ACTN3, MYL1, and MYO18B protein.
